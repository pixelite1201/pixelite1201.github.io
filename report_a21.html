Keyboard Shortcuts:

Modes:
  • I ---Initial Position for all parts
  • | ---Human
  • [ ---Box
  • s ---Scene
  • t ---Torso
  • n ---Neck
  • h ---Head
  • q ---Left Upper Arm
  • w ---Left Lower Arm
  • e ---Left Hand
  • p ---Right Upper Arm
  • o ---Right Lower Arm
  • i ---Right Hand
  • z ---Left Upper Leg
  • c ---Left Lower Leg
  • a ---Left Foot
  • m ---Right Upper Leg
  • b ---Right Lower Leg
  • l ---Right Foot
*Legs and arms shortcut are related to keyboard layout of alphabets.

Rotation Controls:

  • 0 ---Initial position for selected part
  • 1,2 ---X axis
  • 3,4 ---Y axis
  • 5,6 ---Z axis
  • 7,8 ---Open/close box
